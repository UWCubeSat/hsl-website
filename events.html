Husky Satellite Lab Events

General Meetings

General meetings are where the entire club discusses logistics and plans technical integration. They're open to all interested students, not just members.

In the Autumn quarter of 2024, general meetings are held every other Wednesday starting on October 30th, at 6:00-7:00 PM in Miller Hall 301.

Annual Events

At the beginning of each academic year, Husky Satellite Lab participates in events such as Engineering Launch. Find out about specific events on the Dawg Daze website!

Subteam Meetings

Husky Satellite Lab subteams organize their own meetings to do subsystem-specific work. These are only open to current Husky Satellite Lab members. Times are posted on the internal Slack and internal Google Calendar.
